One of the most convenient ways to spend your Bitcoin is by using a prepaid debit card.

Bitpay offers a prepaid Visa card for $9.95.

You pay for the card with Bitcoin, and fund it from your Bitcoin Wallet.

There's no credit check, and the card is delivered to you in about a week.

The card is accepted wherever Visa is accepted.

You can link your card to Paypal, and transfer funds to your bank.

You can also withdraw funds from an ATM, and get cash.
